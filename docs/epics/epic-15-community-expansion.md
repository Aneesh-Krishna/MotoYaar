# Epic 15 — Community Expansion

**Source:** [growth-features-outcomes.md](../growth-features-outcomes.md)
**Priority:** P2–P3
**Created:** 2026-04-28
**Depends On:** Epics 01–03, 09 (foundation, vehicles, community)

---

## Epic Goal

Deepen the community's utility by adding vehicle-contextual content: a mechanic review layer (crowdsourced POI) and a vehicle history lookup (used-buyer hook). Both features become more valuable as user count grows — build them when early retention signals are confirmed.

---

## Stories

| Story | Title | Priority | Depends On |
|-------|-------|----------|-----------|
| 15.1 | Mechanic / Service Center Reviews (UGC) | P2 | Epic 05 (expense — service center tagged on service expense) |
| 15.2 | Vehicle History by Registration Number | P3 | Epic 03 (vehicles), Epic 05 (expenses) |

---

## Build Order

15.1 before 15.2. Vehicle history (15.2) is more impactful when the community has a meaningful number of logged service records, which 15.1 helps build.
